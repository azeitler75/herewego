How to create a YouTube video

Step 1 Gathering Material

For me gathering photos and videos is not a problem, I enjoy capturing special moments. If you are having issues; pick an event that you enjoy, grab your smartphone, and set out on an adventure.

Step 2 Upload Material

Depending on how large your files are, you might want to use Dropbox to transfer your files from your phone to your desktop. From your smartphone log into Dropbox and upload your material. If you do not have Dropbox Click Here!

Step 3 Download Material

I prefer to use my desktop when editing. On your desktop navigate to the web and log in to Dropbox. Pull all the material you want to use from Dropbox and add it to a folder on your desktop.

Step 4 Edit Photos

I am comfortable with Photo Gallery for editing that came installed on my desktop. My two favorite buttons to use are Auto Adjust and Crop. There are several other options to use in this program so take the time to explore and have fun. Next, make sure all the edited photos have been saved to your desktop.

Step 5 Choose a Software

There are so many choices of video-making software on the market, I selected Canva. I have a friend that creates videos and they like this program. I was impressed with the software and its abilities, to download canva Click Here!. There is also an app that I downloaded on my smartphone.

Step 6 Start a Project

Once your account is created you can now start a new project, I want to create a video so I selected video. I uploaded all my edited pictures from my desktop and then my unedited video. I was able to select a theme, background, and music from their approved selection. I do pay $12.99 a month for this service, this helps me to avoid any copyright issues.

Step 7 Edit Video

Canva makes editing easy, once I got the hang of how the program runs it was a breeze to edit. The purple area is the start and end of the saved video, the washed-out area on the outer sides will be deleted. You can move the bar around with your mouse to select the area you wish to choose. I had about 8 minutes of video that went down to less than 6 minutes.

Step 8 Save and Create File

Once you are satisfied with your video it's time to download as an MP4 video. On the upper right side select download, then make sure you have the MP4 video selected and all the slides. Save the file on your desktop.

Step 9 Upload to YouTube

I currently have a YouTube account, if you do not Click Here! to create one. Once logged in navigate to your channel by clicking the icon on the upper right side.

Select manage videos, then create and finally upload video. Find the video on your desk top and open it up.

Step 10 Share

On your YouTube home screen find the video you just downloaded click on it.

On the bottom right corner, you will see a share button, click on it. Copy the link and add it to your favorite social media. I selected Facebook and posted it to my page.

Congratulations, YOU DID IT
