Revisions to this site

How I upgraded

WOW! 6 exciting weeks have passed by and it is hard to believe that I just deployed my 1st website. First, thanks for this rare opportunity to participate in the Learn to Code program. I was amazed that I made a website in such a short amount of time, think of what I could do if I had more time.

I asked my good friend Melissa to look at my website for spelling and grammar errors. She is very good at what she does and I appreciated it. We then sat down with a printed copy of my website and edited everything. Melissa and I both agreed that the site was pretty plain and needed some color. I started to revise by typing all the styles individually on each page. Then I paused what am I doing, I need to create a style.css doc. On my revision I added one, I had no idea how easy life can be when you use a style doc. I played around with all sorts of colors, this helped me see what I was changing on the website. It went so quickly; I did some editing, scaled back on the colors, and voila I created my final draft.
